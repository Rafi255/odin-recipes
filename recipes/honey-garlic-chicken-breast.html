Honey Garlic Chicken Breast

Honey Garlic Chicken Breast is a flavorful dish featuring tender chicken breast cooked to golden perfection, then coated in a rich, sweet-savory sauce made from honey, minced garlic, soy sauce, and a touch of vinegar. The taste is perfectly balanced—sweet, salty, and slightly tangy—making it ideal to pair with steamed rice or vegetables. A simple yet satisfying choice for lunch or dinner..

BONUS: This can be made with any protein – beef, pork, lamb, shrimp/prawns, even tofu!

What you need

  • of course a chicken
  • flour
  • garlic
  • vinegar
  • honey
  • soy sauce
  • butter

How to make Honey Garlic Chicken Breast

  1. Split chicken breast in half – so 2 breasts makes 4 pieces;
  2. Dust chicken with flour (gives the chicken a slight crust so the sauce clings to it better!)
  3. Sear chicken, add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey.
  4. Simmer until the sauce thickens. Turn chicken to coat in the amazing honey garlic sauce.

and that's its!

Notice how the sauce is clinging to the surface? That’s because we dusted the chicken with flour so when it’s pan fried, it creates a crust.

What to serve with Honey Garlic Chicken Breast

There’s enough sauce to drizzle over veggies and soak into rice or whatever you serve it with. The flavour is strong enough such that you don’t need loads – a bit goes a long way! It’s pictured above with Cauliflower Rice (tossed with a squeeze of lemon, parsley, salt and pepper) with Cucumber Salad on the side. Even though it’s got soy sauce in the Honey Garlic Sauce, this doesn’t taste specifically Asian-y. But it’s versatile enough that it will go just as well with Fried Rice as it will with a Macaroni Salad or even Mac and Cheese. Yes really! Try it once and you’ll understand (it only takes 12 minutes, you’ve got the time!!)
